InvestIQ Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

InvestIQ ("we", "the app") is an educational app that helps you explore investing literacy topics. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Summary

We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. There are no user accounts, no email sign-up, and no advertising identifiers.

Data stored on your device

The app saves the following locally on your phone using device storage only:

  • Your quiz result (investing persona)
  • Whether you completed the quiz
  • Glossary terms you bookmark

This data never leaves your device unless you uninstall the app or use "Retake the quiz" to clear it.

Data we do not collect

  • Name, email, phone number, or address
  • Location
  • Financial account or payment information
  • Analytics that identify you
  • Data sent to our servers (we do not operate backend servers for this app)

Third parties

The app does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs. It does not provide brokerage, trading, or personalized financial advice.

Children

InvestIQ is intended for general audiences learning about investing concepts. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date above will change when we do.
